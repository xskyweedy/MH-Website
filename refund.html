Midas Host's Refund Policy.

This page was updated on November 27TH, 2023

In fact, like all other companies, we could add many unnecessary procedures and conditions here. But we want all our customers who prefer Midas Host to be extremely satisfied and happy. If you have any problem with your server, you can get a refund within 3 DAYS without any reason.

You can only get a refund within the first 3 days of the "first order" date. It is not valid for the first 3 days of the following (recurring) months and is non-refundable.



How to get a refund?


By contacting via email, ticket panel or ticker discord; Tell our technical support team that you would like to receive a refund. Your server will then be "permanently and irreversibly" deleted, and your money (100%) will be refunded to your card without processing fee. Refunds may take several days, depending on your bank.
